What Will I Learn in JCCC's Web Development and Digital Media Program

Start a conversation or tell a story using the language of technology. With just a few semesters of study, you'll gain deeper understanding of the digital workflow, web applications, web development and content management - and be ready to launch your new career in web development and digital media.

Work in both Windows and Mac to build your toolbox. Start the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Move on to web scripting for front-end and back-end design and development using PHP, UX, UI, Bootstrap, jQuery and React. Get experience building in WordPress and Drupal content management systems.

In our Web Development and Digital Media courses, you'll learn:

  • Digital Media Conccepts
  • Digital workflow
  • Web Analytics
  • Visual Story Telling
  • Search engine optimization (SEO)
  • User Experience
  • Content and digital asset creation
  • Emerging technologies
  • Web scripting
